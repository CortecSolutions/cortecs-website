How to Find Your Size

Follow these simple steps to get the perfect fit

BEFORE YOU START

Prepare Your Garment

✓
Use a similar garment you already own that fits well
✓
Ensure all buttons and zippers are closed
✓
Lay your garment flat with the front facing down

Step 1 — Width (A)

Measure Side Seam to Side Seam

"Approximately 2 inches below the armpit"

B
Step 2 — Length (B)

Measure Collar to Hem

"From the bottom of the collar to the edge of the bottom hem"
