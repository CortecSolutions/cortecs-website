BEFORE YOU START

Prepare Your Garment

✓
Use a garment you already own that fits well
✓
Ensure all buttons and zippers are closed
✓
Lay it flat with the front facing down
STEP 1
A — Width

Measure Side to Side

Measure from side seam to side seam, approximately 2 inches below the armpit.

Write down this measurement in inches.
STEP 2
B — Length

Measure Collar to Hem

Measure from the bottom of the collar down to the bottom hem of the garment.

This gives you the overall length.
STEP 3

Find Your Size
