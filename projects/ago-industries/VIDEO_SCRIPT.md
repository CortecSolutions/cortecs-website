# AGO Industries - Sizing Guide Video Script

## Video Overview
**Title:** How to Find Your Perfect Fit at AGO Industries
**Duration:** 60-90 seconds
**Style:** Professional, clean, instructional
**Branding:** Blue & white color scheme, AGO logo

---

## SCENE BREAKDOWN

### SCENE 1: Intro (5 seconds)
**Visual:** AGO Industries logo animation, blue background
**Text on screen:** "Find Your Perfect Fit"
**Voiceover/Text:** "Getting the right size is easy when you follow these simple steps."

---

### SCENE 2: What You'll Need (5 seconds)
**Visual:** Flat surface with items appearing
**Items shown:**
- A well-fitting garment you already own
- A measuring tape
- The AGO sizing chart

**Text on screen:** "What you'll need"

---

### SCENE 3: Prepare Your Garment (10 seconds)
**Visual:** Hands laying out a hi-vis safety shirt on a flat surface

**Steps shown:**
1. Choose a garment that fits you well
2. Button or zip all closures
3. Lay it flat with the FRONT FACING DOWN

**Text on screen:** "Step 1: Prepare your garment"
**Voiceover:** "Start with a garment that fits you comfortably. Make sure all buttons and zippers are closed, then lay it flat with the front facing down."

---

### SCENE 4: Measure Width (15 seconds)
**Visual:** Top-down view of garment, measuring tape being placed

**Action:**
- Camera shows garment from above
- Animated line appears showing measurement points
- Measuring tape stretches from side seam to side seam
- Text shows "2 inches below armpit"
- Measurement number appears (e.g., "24 inches")

**Text on screen:** "Step 2: Measure Width"
**Highlight:** "Side seam to side seam, 2" below the armpit"

**Voiceover:** "Measure the width from side seam to side seam, about 2 inches below the armpit. Write down this number."

---

### SCENE 5: Measure Length (15 seconds)
**Visual:** Side view transitioning to measurement

**Action:**
- Measuring tape runs from collar/top to bottom hem
- Animated line traces the path
- Measurement number appears (e.g., "32 inches")

**Text on screen:** "Step 3: Measure Length"
**Highlight:** "From collar to hem"

**Voiceover:** "Next, measure the length from the collar or top of the garment down to the bottom hem."

---

### SCENE 6: Pants Measurement (10 seconds)
**Visual:** Work pants laid flat

**Action:**
- Show waist measurement (across the top)
- For inseam: inside leg from crotch to hem

**Text on screen:** "For Pants: Measure waist width and inseam"

---

### SCENE 7: Compare to Chart (15 seconds)
**Visual:** Split screen - measurements on left, AGO size chart on right

**Action:**
- Animated finger/pointer moves across chart
- Highlights matching row
- Size "LARGE" or similar lights up

**Text on screen:** "Step 4: Find your size"
**Voiceover:** "Now compare your measurements to the AGO sizing chart. Find the row that matches your numbers - that's your size!"

---

### SCENE 8: Pro Tips (10 seconds)
**Visual:** Quick tips appearing as icons/text

**Tips:**
- "Between sizes? Go up for comfort"
- "Consider layering for cold weather"
- "Questions? Contact AGO at 519-452-3780"

**Text on screen:** "Pro Tips"

---

### SCENE 9: Outro (5 seconds)
**Visual:** AGO logo, contact info

**Text on screen:**
- AGO Industries
- www.ago1.com
- 519-452-3780

**Voiceover:** "AGO Industries - Safety apparel that fits."

---

## TOTAL RUNTIME: ~90 seconds
